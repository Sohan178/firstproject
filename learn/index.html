CIVIC CONNECT

CivicConnect is a platform that enables users to report local issues and receive timely assistance. Our main goal is to bridge the gap between citizens and authorities, providing a safe and convenient way to discuss problems without hesitation.

Users can report issues by specifying the location, type of issue, and a brief description. All submissions are securely stored in our database. Administrators review these reports and take appropriate action to help the community efficiently.

To report your issue, you must sign in and login to our page. Our main objective is to address your problem and solve it.
